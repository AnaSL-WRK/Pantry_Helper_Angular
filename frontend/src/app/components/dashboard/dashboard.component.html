What you can do

Click to view your permissions.

⌄

You are in read-only mode.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You cannot add, edit, or delete pantry items.
  • You cannot mark food as consumed or wasted.
  • You cannot create, edit, or delete recipes.
  • You cannot manage the household or its members.

You can help track food usage.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can mark pantry items as consumed or wasted.
  • You cannot add, edit, or delete pantry items.
  • You cannot create, edit, or delete recipes.
  • You cannot manage the household or its members.

You can manage pantry stock, but not the household itself.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can add, edit, and delete pantry items.
  • You can mark pantry items as consumed or wasted.
  • You cannot create, edit, or delete household recipes.
  • You cannot manage household details or members.

You have full household access.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can add, edit, and delete pantry items.
  • You can mark pantry items as consumed or wasted.
  • You can create, edit, and delete household recipes.
  • You can manage the household - edit details, add members, change roles, and remove members.
